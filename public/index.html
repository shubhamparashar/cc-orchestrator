Open on your phone

First time? Set up Tailscale (private access)
On your Mac
  1. Install Tailscale: brew install --cask tailscale — or download it from tailscale.com/download / the Mac App Store.
  2. Open the Tailscale app and sign in (Google, GitHub, Microsoft, or email). Note which account you use.
  3. In the cc-orchestrator repo, run ./phone-link.sh. This serves this dashboard over your private tailnet on HTTPS.
  4. Reopen this panel — the transport above flips to Tailscale and a one-tap link appears.
On your phone
  1. Install Tailscale from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
  2. Sign in with the same account you used on the Mac — both devices must be on the same tailnet.
  3. Turn the Tailscale toggle on. iOS will ask to add a VPN configuration — allow it.
  4. Back here, copy the one-tap link (AirDrop or Message it to yourself) and open it on the phone. It signs you in automatically.

Tailscale is a private WireGuard mesh — only your own signed-in devices can reach the link. Nothing is exposed to the public internet, and the link stops working the moment Tailscale is off.
